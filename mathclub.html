Competition Rules & Submission Guidelines

Rules

  1. Open to KU undergraduates.
  2. Your solution will be graded with respect to correctness, exposition quality, presentation quality (typesetting in LaTex preferred), and the elegance of the solution.
  3. Solutions will be ranked and the top submission will receive a prize and the associated glory. Up to three honorable mentions will also receive a small prize. Winners will be announced, and prizes distributed, at Math Club meetings.

Submission guidelines

  • Email your solution as an attachement to Reuven Hodges rmhodges@ku.edu with the subject line "Math Club problem of the month solution".
  • Your solution should preferably be a pdf (the only other acceptable format is an image file).
